Awareness is impersonal, and universal

This realisation was the most profound insight of my life. I was in the shower, and broadening my awareness when a question spontaneously popped into my head: “What if everyone feels not just similarly to you, but exactly the same as you. What if you’ve been walking round you whole life considering your awareness to somehow be uniquely Gerry, when in fact the core experiences of everyday life will feel exactly the same to everyone.”

This blew my mind, not just in a cognitive way, but in a felt way. I didn’t simply know the answer, I knew it. It was a beautiful light bulb moment,
